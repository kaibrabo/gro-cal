FAQs

How is the end date calculated?

You input your plant's flip date and flowering duration. Blumelist does the math and gives you an estimated harvest date.

What counts as an "active" plant?

Any plant that hasn’t been marked as finished or harvested is considered active.

Is there a mobile app?

Blumelist is a responsive web app — you can access it on any device with a browser.
